BICE Mare Dining Club

BiCE Mare, an award winning fine dining Italian restaurant located at Souq Al Bahar with a stunning views of Burj Khalifa and the Dubai Fountain Show, is proud to introduce a first of its kind and super exclusive

“Bice Mare Dining Club” which offers you a memorable 24 dinners or lunches valued at 12,000 dirham only for 6000 thus giving you a total saving of nearly 50% off.

The membership allows you to receive 24  sumptuous three course meals (starter, main and desert) that you can consume at any time over the next 12 months which you can utilize in any given visit partially or fully and enjoy it along with your family or  guests until you fully consume these 24 meals within 12 months from your purchase date.

Do not miss this exciting and very limited offer which will ends in two weeks from now.

Click on the bellow link to purchase the above membership online (we also offer installment plan) or just to register your interest and be called back with an invitation to visit us and sign up in person instead.

T & C: Membership meals cannot be redeemed on February 14 or December 31. The 24 meals must be consumed within 12 months from the date of activation.
